Autonomous Driving Using Imitation Learning

  • Comparing Effectiveness of Different Network Structures in Imitation Learning (on going)

  • Avoidance of Manual Labeling in Robotic Autonomous Navigation Through Multi-Sensory Semi-Supervised Learning

    Navigation and recording policies are parameterized by a CNN and a two-layer fully connected network.
    iRobot Create2 mounted with Nvidia Jetson TX1 with Zed stereo camera and ultrasonic sensors

    Using imitation learning in real-world robot navigation task without human correction. Our proposed framework allows the robot to learn from a suboptimal policy derived from imperfect sensor data without overfitting. This suboptimal policy eliminates the need of human correction in DAgger algorithm. In addition, the recording policy constrains the robot learning from the suboptimal policy. In the experiment, we show that the framework can achieve near human performance in most of the tasks and even surpasses human performance in case of unexpected events.

Full Duplex Wireless Interference Cancellation Using Machine Learning

  • Realtime Software Defined Self-Interference Cancellation Based on Machine Learning for In-Band Full Duplex Wireless Communications

    The proposed method uses a fully connected neural network implemented in tensorflow to predict self-interference in digital domain. There is nearly 50 dB self-interference cancelled at 2500.150MHz after the training process which surpasses many baseline methods.
